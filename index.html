In the past one year, I had the oppertunity to learn and create come projects in unity. Each and every project made me learn new concepts and features of unity. The following are some of my projects that helped me gain Experience im some core concepts and elements in unity.

Multiplayer Escape room in Meta VR

A captivating and immersive Multiplayer Escape Room experience in Virtual Reality specifically designed for the Meta Quest 2 platform. Leveraging my expertise in Unity, I harnessed the power of the XR Interaction Toolkit to craft a seamless and intuitive interaction system, allowing users to effortlessly engage with the virtual environment. Through this toolkit, I implemented an innovative grabbing and interaction mechanism that enhances immersion and interactivity within the virtual world.
To ensure a seamless multiplayer experience, I adeptly integrated Photon PUN (Photon Unity Networking) into the project. My proficient utilization of Photon PUN facilitated the synchronization of the virtual environment across multiple devices and players. This synchronization extended to encompass not only the environment itself but also the dynamic interactions and movements of the players. This attention to detail and synchronization contributes significantly to the realism and engagement of the multiplayer escape room experience.
Furthermore, I demonstrated my prowess in networking by seamlessly integrating a voice chat feature using Photon Voice. This feature fosters real-time communication between players, enhancing teamwork and collaboration within the escape room scenario. Through meticulous coding and integration of the voice chat system, I ensured clear and reliable communication that further enriched the overall multiplayer experience.

Augmented Race track

A dynamic and innovative mobile Racing game that seamlessly integrates real-world images as customizable tracks. I engineered a cutting-edge gameplay concept that empowers users to craft personalized racetracks by aligning images in various combinations. This inventive approach offers players an unparalleled level of creativity and engagement as they construct unique tracks to race through on their mobile devices.
For the implementation of this groundbreaking concept, I adeptly utilized the Vuforia Engine, a powerful augmented reality tool. I leveraged its advanced image tracking capabilities to seamlessly blend virtual elements with real-world images, spawning interactive game elements and constructing an augmented environment that truly pushes the boundaries of immersive gameplay. By skillfully integrating the Vuforia Engine, I ensured that the augmented reality components seamlessly interact with the user's chosen images, enhancing the overall gaming experience.
Furthermore, I demonstrated my mastery in game mechanics and data management by implementing a comprehensive highscore table. Utilizing JSON (JavaScript Object Notation) files as the storage medium, I proficiently recorded and managed vital game data, including the time taken to complete each track, the user's name, and the corresponding track name. My adept handling of JSON allowed for efficient data storage and retrieval, ensuring a smooth and responsive experience for players as they compete to achieve the best times on various tracks.

A Combat game with switching characters

I led the development of a captivating PC game that offers a truly unique and immersive experience. In this game, players find themselves locked in a relentless struggle against an AI-controlled monster, where the roles of protagonist and antagonist interchange unpredictably.
By leveraging my skills in game design and programming, I orchestrated the creation of this innovative gameplay concept that seamlessly alternates control between the player and the AI monster. The crux of this experience lies in the fluid switch between these roles, introducing an element of unpredictability and intensity that keeps players engrossed.
Central to this experience is the strategic intertwining of gameplay mechanics. The player's objective is to strategically eliminate the enemy while in control of the hero character. This dynamic is heightened by the association of control with the concept of a soul and the concept of health with the body. This innovative mechanic introduces an added layer of strategy, as players must not only maneuver their way through the challenges presented but also choose the optimal moments to initiate the final confrontation.
The game's core mechanic—the alternating control—drives the narrative forward, fostering a thrilling sense of urgency and anticipation. This alternation is governed by a cleverly devised mechanism: a random switching time influenced by a rage gauge that gradually diminishes. This strategic balance of timing and control adds depth to the gameplay, ensuring that players remain on edge while adapting to the ever-changing dynamic.
